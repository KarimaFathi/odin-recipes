Moroccan Desserts : Harcha

Description

Harcha is a famous Moroccan dessert that you can enjoy either with tea or a warm cofe

  • 2 Two cups of semolina
  • 1 cup of water
  • pinch of salt
  • ½ cup of oil
  • ½ teaspoon of sugar
  • ½ teaspoon of Baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the oil and mix the ingredients with your hands.
  3. Gradually add water and knead with your hands until you get a soft dough.
  4. Sprinkle a little semolina over the dough.
  5. Place the dough in a suitable pan and flatten it slightly with the palms of your hands. Leave it on the fire until it is well cooked and turns golden..
  6. Flip the harcha onto a plate, then return it to the pan to cook on the other side.
