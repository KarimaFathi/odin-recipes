Moroccan Desserts : Baghrir

Description

Baghrir is a famous Moroccan dessert that you can enjoy either with tea or a warm cofe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semolina
  • 1 cup white flour
  • 2 bags of sweet yeast
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 4 cups warm water
  • 1 cup honey

Steps

  1. Put in a large bowl: semolina, white flour, sweet yeast, salt, and yeast.
  2. Add water to the previous ingredients, then mix this mixture well.
  3. Leave the mixture for 15 minutes until it rests.
  4. Bring the special baghrir frying pan, turn on the fire, and use a large ladle to distribute the mixture well.
  5. Repeat the previous step until the quantity runs out.
  6. Serve the baghrir immediately with a cup of honey and milted butter in a serving dish.
